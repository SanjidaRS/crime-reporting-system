Q1. Can people report anonymously?

Answer:
Yes, name/email/phone are optional.

---

Q2. Can someone submit multiple reports?

Answer:
Yes.

---

Q3. What happens after submitting?

Answer:
The report is saved with status Pending, and the user receives a unique Report ID.

Answer:
No (Version 1).

Answer:
Yes.
